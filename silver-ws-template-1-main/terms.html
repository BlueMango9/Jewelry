Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

1. Pristine Silver Metal Valuations

All product listings and prices provided on this website are indicative. Due to the high-value precious metals and fluctuations in the daily gold/silver spot market bullion values, exact sales prices are computed dynamically on the day of the customer consultation and order booking.

2. Enquiry-Only Workflow Policy

This website does not possess any payment gateways, checkout processes, or shopping carts. Adding products to a Wishlist constitutes a client request for quotation. No transactions can or will be charged through this online domain. Bookings are handled individually by a dedicated precious metal expert via direct voice calls, video consults, or WhatsApp chats.

3. Custom Artisanship Orders

Requests for custom weights, solid castings, hollow deities, or specific regional filigree work are subject to specific artisan timelines. Once a custom design diagram and weight are agreed upon, we require a safety deposit prior to starting the production. Timelines generally range between 2 to 6 weeks.

4. Data Privacy Safeguards

Your personal data (Name, Phone number, Email address, and Wishlist items) shared through our contact forms is exclusively processed to manage your custom silver quotes. We maintain strict privacy and never share, trade, or distribute your private contact details to third-party advertising registries.
